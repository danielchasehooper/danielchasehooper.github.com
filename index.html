Favorite Books

  • The Visual Display of Quantitative Information By Edward R. Tufte
  • Understanding Comics: The Invisible Art By Scott McCloud
  • The Art of Doing Science and Engineering By Richard W. Hamming
  • The Little Schemer By Daniel P. Friedman and Matthias Felleisen
  • The Animator's Survival Kit By Richard Williams
  • About Face 3: The Essentials of Interaction Design By Alan Cooper
  • The Elements of Computing Systems by Noam Nisan
  • Butterick's Practical Typography By Matthew Butterick
  • The Inmates are Running the Asylum By Alan Cooper
  • The Humane Interface By Jef Raskin
  • The Elements of Friendly Software Design By Paul Heckel
